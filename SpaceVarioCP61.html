SpaceVario CP61 provides dependent-access parking lifts with clear height down to 10' 6". The most cost-effective stacked parking for driver-operated tandem stalls, while also the most popular valet-operated solution.

The SpaceVario CP61 system offers exceptional value through low capital, operating, and maintenance costs. With hydraulic operation, the system provides superior safety and reliability compared to traditional chain-driven systems.

Built with top-quality components and precision manufacturing, the SpaceVario CP61 provides long service life and exceptional intrinsic value. Full-system hot-dip galvanization and powder coating ensure decades of corrosion-free operation.
