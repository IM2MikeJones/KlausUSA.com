ParkBoards increase parking without needing pits or extra clear height. Tandem spaces become independently driver accessible without a valet.

The ParkBoard PQ system converts traditional tandem "valet" parking stalls into independently accessible, self-parked stalls. This eliminates the need for drive aisles and allows for maximum parking density in existing garage spaces.

With full-system hot-dip galvanization, the ParkBoard PQ provides decades of corrosion-free operation. The system features top-quality components and precision manufacturing for long service life, while maintaining low capital, operating, and maintenance costs.
