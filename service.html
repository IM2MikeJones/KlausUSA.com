SERVICE FAQ

Systems are configured specifically for your building. Instructions for operating your system are posted on a sign above the operating panel. This is typically mounted to a column directly in front of your system.

- Read the instructions on your sign before operating the system -

If you have any questions about operation, please contact your HOA or building management.

If the system is not responding to your remote control, check the batteries or try using your keyfob (the keyfob does not require batteries). Please note that the remote should not be aimed at the control panel. Point your remote at the receivers above the doors.

Make sure to check the screen for errors or instructions to follow.

If the system has an error that cannot be cleared, please call our service department at:

(925) 284-2092 ext:2

Contact your HOA or building manager. They may have a replacement in stock.

Click the button below to purchase keyfobs and remotes. They are preprogrammed in our factory for your stall. Please specify the stall number and the address where the system is installed when ordering.
